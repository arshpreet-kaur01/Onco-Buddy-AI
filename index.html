About Onco-Buddy AI

Onco-Buddy AI was conceptualized to solve an urgent challenge in healthcare engineering: bridge the continuous assistance gap existing between a patient's active cycles and outpatient home-care timelines.

By aggregating automated geolocation diagnostics, responsive multi-lingual architecture, clinical data vault monitoring, and responsive layouts, this portal equips users with verified info powered directly by verified literature metrics.
